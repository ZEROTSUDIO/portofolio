Case Study

Rapa Cast Stone.

Building a scalable, high-performance e-catalogue for the precast concrete industry.

Executive Summary

Rapa Cast Stone is a leading provider of custom precast concrete solutions. They needed a digital transformation to move from offline PDF catalogues to a dynamic, searchable, and SEO-optimized web platform. I served as the lead developer, building a full-stack solution from the ground up.

The Challenge

The primary technical challenge was managing a vast inventory of stone products with varying specifications (weight, dimensions, textures) while ensuring the interface remained extremely fast for mobile users on construction sites.

Key requirements included:

  • Instant search and filtering across 500+ product variants.
  • Easy-to-use Admin Panel for non-technical staff to update inventory.
  • Premium aesthetic that reflects the "luxury" aspect of cast stone architecture.

Technical Solution

I chose the **TALL stack** (Tailwind, Alpine.js, Laravel, Livewire) for its ability to create reactive interfaces with a robust backend.

Backend Architecture

The backend was built on Laravel 10. I implemented a polymorphic database schema to handle different product types efficiently. For the search functionality, I used Livewire component-based state management to provide real-time results without page reloads.
